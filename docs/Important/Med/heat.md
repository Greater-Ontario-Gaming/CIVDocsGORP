<h1>Heat Stroke</h1>

<h2>Symtoms</h2>

- High body temps
- Altered mental state
- Alteration in sweating
- Nausea and vomiting
- Flushed skin
- Rapid breathing
- Racing heart rate
- Headache
- Blurred or double vision

<h2>Causes</h2>

- Exposure to a hot enviroment
- Strenuous activity
- Wearing excess clothing
- Becoming dehydrated
- Drinking alcohol

<h2>Results Without Intervention</h2>

- Vital organ damage
- Death
