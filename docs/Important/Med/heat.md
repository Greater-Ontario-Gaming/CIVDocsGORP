# Heat Stroke

## Symtoms
- High body temps
- Altered mental state
- Alteration in sweating
- Nausea and vomiting
- Flushed skin
- Rapid breathing
- Racing heart rate
- Headache
- Blurred or double vision

## Causes
- Exposure to a hot enviroment
- Strenuous activity
- Wearing excess clothing
- Becoming dehydrated
- Drinking alcohol

## Results Without Intervention
- Vital organ damage
- Death
